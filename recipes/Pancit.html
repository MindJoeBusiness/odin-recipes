Pancit Recipe

Description

This will take about 5-10 minutes to make. Be careful when boiling water

Ingredients

  • 2 packets Pancit Canton Chilimansi flavor Lucky Me brand
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot
  2. Take out the packets inside the pancit package and set them aside
  3. Pour the uncooked noodles inside the pot and wait 5 minutes
  4. Strain the water from the pancit and pour them in a bowl
  5. Add the packets and mix thoroughly
