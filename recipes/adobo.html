Adobo Recipe

Description

The recipe will take 20-25 minutes to cook. Do not rush to make sure the meat becomes soft enough to cook.

Ingredients

  • 10lb pork butt boneless
  • 1/2 cup of cooking oil
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper, sugar and salt
  • Bay leaves
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar

Steps

  1. Pour oil in high heat pot
  2. Simmer garlic until golden brown
  3. Put pork cuts in hot oil and wait 20 minutes while stirring thoroughly every 5 minutes
  4. Add 2 cups of soy sauce and 1 cup vinegar
  5. Add 1 cup of sugar, 3 tbsp salt and 2 tbsp black pepper
  6. Add 5-6 bay leaves and wait to boil
  7. After boil, dish is done!
