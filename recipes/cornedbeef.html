Corned Beef Recipe

Description

The recipe will take about 10-15 mins to make. Make sure to have a comfortable space when cooking because it might get messy.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • onion
  • potatoes
  • 12oz Corned beef in can
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 oil
  • egg(optional)

Steps

  1. Pour oil on medium heat pan
  2. Cook potatoes until it turns golden brown
  3. Take out potatoes and put garlic and onions in the hot oil
  4. Pour 12oz corned beef once garlic and onion is brownish
  5. Mix thoroughly and add 1 cup of water after 5 minutes
  6. Add egg in the middle and wait 2 minutes
  7. Pour the dish on a serving tray
  8. Bon apetit!
